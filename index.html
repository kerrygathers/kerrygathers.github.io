Unemployment in the US

Tracing unemployment rates across the US from recession to boom to recession, this map illustrates the volatility of the US economy from 2001 to 2015. Beneath the high-level trends, the map exposes regional and local stories, like stability amid national crisis, or crisis amid national recovery.

Tech: Leaflet JavaScript jQuery Chroma.js PapaParse

Educational Attainment NYC

While inequality in New York City is typically discussed in terms of income, it shows up in myriad other social conditions. This map explores educational attainment levels in a city where neighborhoods in which 8 in 10 adults have finished college are a short train ride from areas where over a quarter of adults have less than a 9th grade education.

Tech: Leaflet JavaScript jQuery Simple Statistics

The Rent Is Too Damn High

Fossil Fuel Power

In spite of intensifying global climate change, 63% of the electricity produced in the U.S. in 2017 was generated by burning fossil fuels. This map shows fossil fuel power plants and their megawatt capacities according to the U.S. Energy Information Administration's Form 923.

Tech: Leaflet JavaScript

Homebuyer's Guide to Denver

When prospective homebuyers evaluate a neighborhood, they have a lot to consider. This application maps three community assets of interest to a particular homebuyer in Denver: food stores, after school programs, and fire stations. A click on the map highlights assets within 1 km of the click.

Tech: Leaflet JavaScript

Amtrak Usage by State & Station

Using data scraped from Amtrak's 2017 State Fact Sheets, this map compares Amtrak ridership at both the state and city levels. Zoom in to activate proportional symbols indicating boardings and alightings at each Amtrak station.

About the Author

I make maps for work, school (shout out to New Maps Plus), and fun.
